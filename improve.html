Murray Bauman

Private Investigator · Conspiracy Theorist

Murray Bauman is a character from the TV series Stranger Things — a sharp-tongued private investigator who lives alone and trusts no one, especially the government. He believes secret organizations have been hiding the truth for decades. Though eccentric and abrasive, his intellect and skills prove invaluable to the group. He speaks Russian fluently and becomes a critical ally to Hopper, Joyce, Nancy, and Jonathan across multiple seasons.

Role in the Story

Murray Bauman plays an important supporting role in Stranger Things. He helps the main characters understand the secret activities connected with the Hawkins laboratory. Murray investigates strange events and believes the government hides the truth. He helps Nancy Wheeler and Jonathan Byers expose the conspiracy, later becoming an ally of Jim Hopper and Joyce Byers. Because he speaks Russian, he helps translate information about the Russian base. Murray also provides comic relief with his sarcastic personality — and always manages to show up exactly when the team needs him most.
