Murray Bauman

Biography

Murray Bauman is a character from the TV series Stranger Things.He is a private investigator and a conspiracy theorist. Murray lives alone and does not trust the government.He believes that many secret organizations hide the truth. In the series he helps Nancy Wheeler and Jonathan Byers with their investigation.He is very intelligent but sometimes behaves in a strange way. Murray also helps Jim Hopper and Joyce Byers.He speaks Russian and helps translate during an important mission.

Role in the story

Murray Bauman plays an important supporting role in the TV series Stranger Things.He helps the main characters understand the secret activities connected with the Hawkins laboratory. Murray investigates strange events and believes that the government hides the truth.He helps Nancy Wheeler and Jonathan Byers expose the conspiracy. Later he becomes an ally of Jim Hopper and Joyce Byers.Because he speaks Russian, he helps translate information about the Russian base. Murray also provides comic relief with his sarcastic personality.Overall, he helps the characters reveal the truth and fight the hidden danger.
